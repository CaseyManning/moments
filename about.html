moments is my journal mostly. casey's journal.

these days i think of a moment as maybe half poem, half journal.

a long time ago moments were just about publishing anything at all, and were more clearly vignettes from my life. i could feel it all slipping through my fingers without moments.

now they are more vignettes of a mental landscape. i think im less interested in concrete happenings, but i still feel very strongly about the moment as a sort of snapshot, a particular way of engaging with my experience of things.
